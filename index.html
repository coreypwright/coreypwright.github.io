About Me

Corey Wright

I currently work with a group of wonderful individuals at Rochester Institute of Technology, providing captioning and interpreting support for over a thousand deaf and hard-of-hearing students on our campus. The problem is that most deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals are not as fortunate as our students when it comes to accessibility options. That is why I’m delighted to expand my horizons and work in a freelance capacity to provide captioning services at an affordable rate to individuals who would otherwise be forced to go without equal access to spoken content.
